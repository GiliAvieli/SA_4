# Requirements

## Functional Requirements & User Stories

| Priority | Classification | User Story | Functional Requirements | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Functional | US1 – As a warehouse staff member, I want to manage the equipment catalog (create, view, update, delete) in order to maintain accurate and up-to-date information on all equipment in the warehouse. | CRUD equipment – Creation, viewing, updating, and deletion of equipment items (item name, category, equipment type, container number). | Not Started |
| 2 | Functional | US2 – As a guide, I want to submit an equipment reservation request and receive automatic availability verification in order to ensure the equipment I need for my activity is available. | 1. The system will enable a guide to submit an equipment reservation request from within the warehouse catalog by clicking a button for a future activity. 2. The system will perform automatic availability checking when requesting equipment and alert if the equipment is not available. | Not Started |
| 2 | Functional | US3 – As a guide, I want to manage equipment reservations in order to plan activities efficiently and track which items are needed. | 1. The system will allow changing the "equipment reservation" that a guide requested. 2. The system will allow viewing the equipment reservation calendar with events and tasks. 3. The system will enable the guide to mark another guide's equipment reservation as "in use" for a different reservation. 4. The system will allow adding items to a guide-reserved equipment list. | Not Started |
| 2 | Functional | US4 – As a warehouse staff member, I want to manage equipment status in order to maintain inventory control and track equipment location and condition. | 1. The system will enable a warehouse staff member to mark "borrowed" status in the warehouse catalog by recording the specific guide's name. 2. The system will provide a warehouse staff member access to an up-to-date list of equipment status (borrowed/in warehouse/missing/under repair/with another troop). 3. The system will enable a warehouse staff member to document the return of reusable equipment after activity completion, including marking the equipment returned, quantity returned, condition status, and updating equipment status in the system. | Not Started |
| 1 | Functional | US5 – As a warehouse staff member, I want to manage consumable inventory including automatic reduction and minimum threshold setting in order to keep inventory available and prevent shortages. | 1. The system will manage consumable inventory and automatically decrease quantities (e.g., 4A paper sheets, flour packages) after use approval. 2. The system will enable defining a "minimum threshold" for consumable equipment (such as toilet paper, matches, rope #4). | Not Started |
| 2 | Functional | US7 – As a junior coordinator, I want the ability to change reserved equipment in order to prioritize equipment allocation based on troop needs. | 1. The system will enable a junior coordinator to change "equipment reservation" that a guide requested. | Not Started |
| 1 | Functional | US8 – As a guide, I want to manage activities (creation, viewing, updating, deletion) in order to plan and maintain educational activities in an organized manner. | CRUD activity – Creation, viewing, updating, and deletion of activities. | Not Started |
| 3 | Functional | US9 – As a guide, I want to manage activity plans in a system that supports real-time updates and change tracking in order to avoid multiple versions. | 1. The system will allow a guide to upload a file for an activity plan (Doc) under the appropriate troop, for approval by a level above. 2. The system will enable a status classification mechanism for activity plans (in progress/approved/rejected). 3. The system will allow a troop leader (RagShaGad) to write notes on the uploaded file for corrections and quality improvement of the activity. 4. The system will provide access to view the content of the corrected activity plan for the guide/troop leader. | Not Started |

The system will display to the troop leader the dates of activity submission by guides and mark who submitted activity plans and who is late. | Not Started |
| 1 | Functional | US12 – As a senior coordinator, I want to manage user profiles (creation, viewing, updating, deletion) in order to maintain current information on senior scout (Shkab"g) members. | CRUD user – Creation, viewing, updating, and deletion of profiles for senior scouts (Shkab"g) (personal details, current role). | Not Started |
| 2 | Functional | US13 – As a junior coordinator, I want to manage guide information (attendance, skills assessment, coaching observations) in order to track performance and professional development. | 1. The system will enable entry of senior scout (Shkab"g) attendance in team meetings, work days, and activities. 2. The system will enable rating proficiency levels according to criteria (e.g., "teaching abilities," "scout proficiency") for each guide in his personal file. 3. The system will enable entry of "coaching observation" (summary of coordinator's visit to an activity). | Not Started |
| 2 | Functional | US14 – As a coordinator, I want to view and filter the assignment preferences of guides in order to make role assignments based on objective criteria. | 1. The system will enable submission of an annual "role preference form" by senior scouts (Shkab"g) in digital form. | Not Started |
| 3 | Functional | US15 – As a senior scout, I want to see a personal calendar with tasks and events in order to stay organized and meet deadlines. | 1. The system will display a personal calendar for each senior scout (Shkab"g) with all events and personal tasks (activity submission deadlines, team meetings). | Not Started |
| 1 | Functional | US16 – As a senior coordinator, I want to manage scout information (creation, updating, viewing, deletion) in order to maintain current and centralized information on all troop scouts. | CRUD scout – Creation, viewing, updating, and deletion of scout details (name, level, parent details, sensitivities). | Not Started |
| 2 | Functional | US17 – As a guide, I want to enter scout attendance in the system and receive reminders to do so in order to enable accurate tracking of scout participation. | 1. The system will enable entry of named attendance for each scout in each activity (present/absent/late). 2. The system will display for each troop the number of scouts who attended the activity. 3. The system will send automatic SMS reminders to guides at the end of an activity to fill in scout attendance in the activity. | Not Started |
| 2 | Functional | US18 – As a senior coordinator, I want to see scout attendance data by troop and troop level in order to get a complete picture of scout participation and support informed decision-making. | 1. The system will display the number of scouts in the troop who attended the activity. | Not Started |
| 3 | Functional | US19 – As a guide, I want to view scout sensitivities and report exceptional incidents in the system in order to ensure scout care, documentation, and follow-up on exceptional circumstances. | 1. The system will enable the report of an exceptional incident (discipline/safety) at the end of an activity linked to the scout's file. 2. The system will enable viewing the sensitivities of scouts under the same guide. | Not Started |
| 1 | Functional | US20 – As a senior coordinator, I want to manage the structure of troops and groups in the system in order to maintain organized troop management. | CRUD troop/group – Creation, viewing, updating, and deletion of troops/groups in the troop. | Not Started |
| 1 | Functional | US22 – As a senior coordinator, I want to receive an objective competency score for senior scouts (Shkab"g) in order to make assignment decisions based on data. | 1. Report "competency score for Shkab"g": Weighting of attendance, timeliness, and feedback scores into an objective role assignment score. | Not Started |
| 1 | Functional | US23 – As a senior coordinator, I want to receive an annual inventory forecast report based on consumption data in order to plan budgets and prepare for future needs of the troop. | 1. Report "annual inventory forecast": Analysis of consumable equipment usage rate throughout the year, combined with data on unreturned equipment and damaged equipment, to forecast required budget for the next year. | Not Started |
| 2 | Functional | US24 – As a system user, I want the system to support 200 concurrent users in order for the system not to crash and withstand loads during periods of troop activities (scout attendance entry, equipment withdrawal - activities that regularly occur at the same times). | 1. The system will work correctly in the browsers Chrome, Safari, Edge. 2. The system will be accessible from a mobile phone. | Not Started |

## Non-Functional Requirements

| Priority | Requirement | Description |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Security | Personal information of scouts and guides must be stored securely. Sensitive data should not be accessible to unauthorized users. |
| 2 | Performance | Response time for operations should not exceed 3 seconds in order for the system to be user-friendly and not freeze during multiple operations. |
| 1 | Scalability | The system must support 200 concurrent users. To ensure the system is comfortable for users and does not crash and withstands loads during periods of troop activities. |
| 1 | Data Backup | Automatic daily backup to cloud. The system will automatically preserve data in order for important data not to be deleted. |
| 1 | Access Control | The system will include permissions by role: guide, troop leader (RagShaGad), coordinator, warehouse staff. Each role holder will be exposed only to user details under him and furthermore, will only be able to perform actions within the scope of his role (for example - a guide cannot order inventory, and warehouse staff cannot fill scout attendance, etc.). |
| 2 | Browser Compatibility | The system will function correctly in Chrome, Safari, Edge browsers. |
| 3 | Localization | The system interface will support Hebrew and English languages. |
| 2 | Audit Trail | The system will preserve a history of performed actions. To identify and analyze the quantity of actions performed in the system. |
| 2 | Offline Capability | The system will support viewing data (attendance/allergies of scouts) without an internet connection. |
